The XLL system is a development for a Web platform, this system will allow gym owners to manage the exercise plans of their clients and the machines that they will use in said plans, allowing an improvement in the time of use of each machine on planes, with the client always having a machine insured for use. In addition, the user (Client) will be able to carry out their training with an exercise plan previously loaded and assisted by a professional, depending only on the system and managing their progress and history from the web.


User characteristics




Type of user: manager
Technical formation: Basic handling of pc, smart tablet or forms. activity professional.
Activities: ABM management of users, products and exercise plans.
Type of user: User
Technical formation: Basic handling of pc, smart tablet or forms.
Activities: See your exercise plans, report if you finished your exercise to release the machine to another user.
Type of user: General
Technical formation: Basic handling of pc, smart tablet or forms.
Activities: You can make your purchase, inform the payment and track the shipment.
